MineBot

MineBot is a Discord bot developed by me in Lua, Luvit and with the help of Discordia API. Though it is a very unstable bot due to most of it libraries being handwritten, it has many useful features and serves approximately 1200 users online.

MineBot RVC

RVC is a relatively new technology. It is able to convert any voice input by the user into other voices, as pre-trained, with natural-sounding voice and even mimicing the target voice's accents and prosody.

RVC uses AI technology to synthesise voice conversion compared to traditional methods of just changing the pitch of the sound given, and/or add extra digital effects to it such as noise cancellation, echo, reverb, etc. RVC gives a more “natural” conversion of voices, even copying their accents and intonations.
